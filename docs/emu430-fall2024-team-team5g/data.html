Data

Data Sources

All datasets are obtained from TÜİK (Türkiye İstatistik Kurumu — Turkish Statistical Institute). The analyses focus on the period 2018–2024, which is the overlapping timeframe for all datasets.

Datasets

1. Provincial In-Migration by Reason (aldigi_goc.xls)

Records the number of people who migrated into each province, broken down by reason for migration (work, education, family, housing, etc.).

  • Sheet: t10
  • Coverage: 81 provinces × 2018–2024
  • Key columns: Year, Province, Total in-migration, and migration reason categories

2. Provincial Out-Migration by Reason (verdigi_goc.xls)

Records the number of people who migrated out of each province, broken down by reason for migration.

  • Sheet: t11
  • Coverage: 81 provinces × 2018–2024
  • Key columns: Year, Province, Total out-migration, and migration reason categories

3. Education Status by Province (egitim.xlsx)

Population aged 6 and above classified by educational attainment level, broken down by province, year, and gender.

  • Sheet: Eğitim Durumu
  • Coverage: 81 provinces × multiple years (2018–2024)
  • Key columns: Year, Province, Education level, Total, Male, Female, Male %, Female %

4. Crime Data — Convicted Persons by Province (suc.xls)

Number of convicted persons entering correctional institutions, broken down by province.

  • Coverage: Multiple years including 2018 onwards
  • Key columns: Province (columns), Year (rows), Count of convicted persons
